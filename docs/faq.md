# Magnu Imperiu FAQ

## What is Magnu Imperiu?

Magnu Imperiu is a free daily puzzle where you identify a historical empire from geographic and chronological clues. One new empire every 24 hours, 6 attempts per round, 50+ empires.

## Who is it for?

Magnu Imperiu is the free Wordle-style history game where you guess a historical empire each day from geographic clues, time period, and comparative size. You get 6 attempts per round, a new empire every 24 hours, and a leaderboard of 50+ empires from Rome to the Ottoman Empire. Great classroom warm-up or coffee break.
