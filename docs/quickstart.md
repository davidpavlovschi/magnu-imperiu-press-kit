# Magnu Imperiu quickstart

Magnu Imperiu is a free daily puzzle where you identify a historical empire from geographic and chronological clues. One new empire every 24 hours, 6 attempts per round, 50+ empires.

## Three steps

1. Open [Magnu Imperiu](https://magnu-imperiu.com)
2. Follow the on-page flow
3. Bookmark https://magnu-imperiu.com for later
